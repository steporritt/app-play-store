Atoms — Privacy Policy

Effective date: 3 July 2026

The short version: Atoms does not collect, store, or share any personal data. At all.

What data Atoms collects

None. Atoms is a turn-based strategy game that runs entirely offline on your device. It does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or any other data about you or your device.

What that means in practice

  • No accounts and no sign-up — you never enter a name, email address, or any other detail.
  • No analytics and no tracking of any kind.
  • No advertising and no advertising identifiers.
  • No in-app purchases.
  • No internet connection is required or used — the game is fully offline.
  • Nothing leaves your device. Game progress (if any) is stored only locally and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Third parties

Because Atoms collects no data, no data is shared with third parties. The app contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking software.

Children

Atoms is an abstract board game suitable for all ages. Since it collects no data from anyone, it collects no data from children.

Changes to this policy

If a future update ever changes what data the game handles (for example, if online multiplayer is added), this policy will be updated first and the effective date above will change.
